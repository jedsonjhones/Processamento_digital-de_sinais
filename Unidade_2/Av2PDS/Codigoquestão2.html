Obtendo os coeficientes do filtro FII

Para Low-freq ((2)a))

fc = 1000; % fc = 1 kHz
wc = fc/(fs/2);
M = 31;
b = fir1(M,wc);

[H,fH] = freqz(b,1,1024,fs);

figure('color','w');
plot(fH/1000,20*log10(abs(H)));
xlabel('f (kHz)')
ylabel('|H| in dB')
title('LowPass Frequency Response')
